# Call with Maria Salazar — 2026-07-14

Maria corrected the *apatheia* reading: it is freedom from the *pathē*, the destructive passions, not the absence of feeling — the Stoics kept the *eupatheiai*. The misreading comes from the English cognate *apathy*. She argued for framing the essay on the dichotomy of control instead, mentioned her current teaching, and shared a plan she asked me not to spread.
