# Frame the essay on the dichotomy of control

On 2026-07-14 I decided to organise the essay around the dichotomy of control from [Stoicism](/concepts/stoicism.md) rather than around *apatheia*.

**Rationale:** the dichotomy lands immediately with a reader who has never opened a Stoic text, while *apatheia* needs a paragraph of disambiguation every time it appears — I misread it myself on the first pass [1], and had to be corrected [2]. An idea that must be defended before it can be used is the wrong load-bearing beam for a short essay.

**Alternatives:** frame it on *apatheia* — richer, and closer to what the Stoics were actually arguing about, but it spends the reader's attention on a correction instead of on the argument. Frame it on the [Epicurean](/concepts/epicureanism.md) contrast — interesting, but it makes the essay comparative rather than practical.

**Status:** active. Revisit if the essay grows long enough to afford the disambiguation.
