# Epicureanism

A Hellenistic school that holds pleasure to be the good — where "pleasure" means *ataraxia*, the absence of disturbance, rather than indulgence. The reputation for hedonism is a later distortion [1].

## Related

- [Stoicism](/concepts/stoicism.md) — contrasted with; both take tranquillity seriously, and disagree on whether the good is virtue or pleasure
